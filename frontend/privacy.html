Privacy Notice

Anonymous Tracking Only

WhoIsThat collects only anonymous analytics to understand visitor patterns. We do not attempt to identify real users.

What We Collect

  • IP address – hashed (SHA-256) before storage. We cannot recover the original IP.
  • Device type – mobile, desktop, or tablet.
  • Browser & OS – from User-Agent (e.g. Chrome 120, Windows 10).
  • Screen resolution – e.g. 1920×1080.
  • Page URL – the page you visited.
  • Referrer – where you came from (or "Direct").
  • Approximate location – country, region, city from IP (not precise).

What We Do Not Collect

  • Names, emails, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • Fingerprints or device IDs
  • Precise geolocation

Purpose

Analytics are used to understand traffic patterns and improve content. Visit counts help distinguish new vs. returning visitors.

Legal & Ethical

This system is designed to be legal and privacy-aware. Use it only on sites you control and where visitors reasonably expect analytics. Comply with applicable laws (e.g. GDPR, CCPA) and disclose tracking in your own privacy policy.
